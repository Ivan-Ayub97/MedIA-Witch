  • Advanced AI Models for Quality Enhancement: Real-ESRGAN, SRGAN, BSRGAN, and IRCNN for enhancing image and video quality, reducing noise, and effectively increasing resolution.

üöÄ Installation

Follow these steps to get started with MedIA-Witch:

  1. Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions.
  2. Launch the application: Run MedIA-Witch.exe on your Windows system.
  3. Start enhancing your images and videos!

MedIA-Witch uses PyInstaller and InnoSetup for easy installation and executable creation.
