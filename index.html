MedIA-Witch

AI-Based Image and Video Enhancement Tool

üåü Description

MedIA-Witch is a powerful open-source desktop application for enhancing images and videos using advanced AI models. Based on QualityScaler, it integrates models such as Real-ESRGAN, SRGAN, BSRGAN, and IRCNN, offering an easy-to-use interface for improving the quality and resolution of your media.

üöÄ Installation

  1. Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions.
  2. Launch the application: Run MedIA-Witch.exe on your Windows system.
  3. Start enhancing your images and videos!

MedIA-Witch uses PyInstaller and InnoSetup for easy installation and executable creation.

üåü Key Features

  • Advanced AI Models for Quality Enhancement: Real-ESRGAN, SRGAN, BSRGAN, and IRCNN for enhancing image and video quality.
  • Batch Processing: Improve multiple images and videos simultaneously.
  • Customizable Settings: Choose output resolution, image/video format, and AI model.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Simplifies media enhancement with just a few clicks.
  • Open-Source: Available under the MIT License, free for modification and extension.
