MedIA-Witch

MedIA-Witch is a powerful open-source desktop application designed to enhance images and videos using advanced AI models. Built upon QualityScaler, the tool integrates optimized models like Real-ESRGAN, SRGAN, BSRGAN, and IRCNN. This allows users to easily improve the quality and resolution of media content with just a few clicks.

  • Advanced AI Models for Quality Enhancement: Features Real-ESRGAN, SRGAN, BSRGAN, and IRCNN for improving image and video quality, reducing noise, and boosting resolution.
  • Batch Processing: Process multiple images and videos simultaneously, perfect for restoring large collections.
  • Customizable Settings: Adjust output resolution, format, and select the optimal AI model for the best enhancement results.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Simple and intuitive interface for users of all levels, making media enhancement easy and fast.
  • Open-Source: MedIA-Witch is released under the MIT License, giving users the freedom to modify and extend the tool's functionality.

üöÄ Installation

To get started with MedIA-Witch, follow these steps:

  1. Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions.
  2. Launch the application: Open MedIA-Witch.exe on your Windows system.
  3. Start enhancing your media!

MedIA-Witch uses PyInstaller and InnoSetup for streamlined installation and packaging.

üõ†Ô∏è How to Use

  1. Launch the application: Run MedIA-Witch.exe as an administrator for optimal performance.
  2. Select your media files: Upload images, videos, or entire folders for enhancement.
  3. Configure your settings: Choose the AI model (Real-ESRGAN, SRGAN, BSRGAN, IRCNN, etc.) and adjust the output resolution and format (PNG, JPEG, MP4, etc.).
  4. Start the enhancement process: Click the UPSCALE button to begin.
  5. Save your enhanced files: Once processing is complete, the files will be saved in your chosen location.
